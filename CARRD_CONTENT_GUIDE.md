## 🎨 Carrd Template Recommendations

When you first go to Carrd.co, you'll see template options. Choose one that:

- ✅ Has a **dark background** (navy, dark gray, charcoal)
- ✅ Has **bright accent colors** (cyan, teal, gold work great)
- ✅ Looks **clean and professional**
- ✅ Has **multiple sections** (not just 1-page)

**Good Options:**
- "Minimal Dark"
- "Modern Dark"
- "Professional Black"
- "Sleek"

Avoid: Ultra-colorful templates, image-heavy templates, or ones with too many distracting elements.

## 🔗 Next: Get Your Typeform Link

Before clicking "Join the Beta" button, you need your Typeform URL:

1. Go to [typeform.com](https://typeform.com)
2. Create a new form
3. Add questions (see Typeform guide below)
4. Copy your form URL
5. Paste it into the Carrd button

---

## 📝 Typeform Questions (in order)

```
1. What's your name? (Short text)

2. What's your email? (Email field)

3. What's your address? (Short text - for location context)

4. Which utilities do you have? (Multiple choice)
   - Electricity only
   - Gas only
   - Electricity + Gas
   - Electricity + Gas + Water
   - Other (please specify)

5. Which utility companies? (Long text)
   Example: "PSE&G (electric/gas) and Veolia (water)"

6. Upload your utility bills (PDF files needed)
   - Can you upload PDFs from the last 3-6 months?
   (File upload field)

7. Why do you want your utility analysis? (Long text)
   - Help us understand what you're most interested in learning

8. Any specific questions about your bills? (Long text)
   - Examples: Why is my bill so high? What uses the most energy?
```
