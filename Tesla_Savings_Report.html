⚡ About Your EV Charging Cost

Your two Tesla Model Ys added approximately $401 (23%) to your 6-month electricity bill.

Your savings vs gas cars: If you had driven two Toyota RAV4s (30 mpg) for the same 8,000 miles/month at NJ gas prices (~$3.00/gal), you would have spent ~$806 on fuel instead of ~$401 on electricity. That's a ~$405 savings over 6 months by using EVs.

Note: This savings is specific to fuel costs. Gas cars also have lower maintenance costs than traditional cars, but EVs have even lower maintenance, providing additional long-term savings.

📅 Seasonal Variations Explained

Summer months (Aug-Sep): Low heating needs (~22 therms gas), minimal cooling (just fans), lower water heating = ~$184-272/month total

Shoulder months (Oct): Increasing heating demand (~44 therms), mild cooling = ~$198/month

Winter months (Nov-Jan): Maximum heating demand (furnace running heavily = 111-140 therms), no cooling, higher water heating for showers = ~$577-802/month total

Water bill: Fairly consistent $49-73/month year-round, slight increase in winter (more showers due to weather).
